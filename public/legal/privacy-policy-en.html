Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Real Estate CRM ("We", "Us", "Platform") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

By using this platform, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you have any questions about our policy, please contact us.

2. Data We Collect

Our platform collects the following personal data:

  • Identity Information: Name, surname, Turkish ID Number (encrypted)
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address
  • Financial Information: IBAN (encrypted), rent amounts, commission information
  • Location Information: Address, city, district, neighborhood information
  • Contract Information: Rental contracts, dates, payment information
  • Authentication Data: Email address, password (securely stored by Supabase Auth)
  • Usage Data: Platform usage statistics, session information

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing property management services
  • Managing tenant and owner information
  • Creating and tracking contracts
  • Sending automated reminders
  • Recording financial transactions
  • Ensuring platform security
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Service improvements and analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected with the following security measures:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data (Turkish ID, IBAN) is protected with AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) database
  • Row Level Security (RLS): Each user can only access their own data
  • HTTPS: All data transfers are made over encrypted connections
  • Regular Backups: Data is regularly backed up
  • Access Control: Data access is strictly controlled

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage services (Data Processor)
  • Exchange Rate APIs: For updating exchange rates

These service providers process your data only to provide our services and are subject to their own privacy policies. We have signed a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with Supabase.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/KVKK)

Under GDPR and KVKK (Turkish Data Protection Law), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Information: Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Right of Access: Request information about your processed personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of incorrect or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Data Portability: Transfer your data to another platform

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Data required to be retained by legal obligations will continue to be stored for the relevant legal period.

8. Cookies

Our platform uses cookies for authentication and session management. For detailed information, please review our Cookie Policy.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or in-platform notification. Changes are effective from the date of publication.
