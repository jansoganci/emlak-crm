Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2025

1. Introduction

The Real Estate CRM platform uses cookies and similar technologies to provide our services and improve user experience. This Cookie Policy explains which cookies are used, why they are used, and how you can manage them.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are saved on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to remember your visit and store your preferences.

3. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of the platform and cannot be disabled.

  • Authentication Cookies: Used for session management and secure login (Supabase Auth)
  • Security Cookies: Used to ensure platform security
  • Preference Cookies: Used for language preference and user settings

Duration: Session duration or 30 days

Performance Cookies

These cookies are used to analyze and improve platform performance.

  • Analytics Cookies: Collect platform usage statistics (currently not used)

Duration: 12 months

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our platform uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Uses cookies for authentication and database services
  • Exchange Rate APIs: For updating exchange rates (does not use cookies)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. For more information about Supabase's cookie policy, please review the Supabase Privacy Policy.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies. You can disable or delete cookies from your browser's settings menu. However, if you disable essential cookies, some features of the platform may not work.

Platform Settings

You can change your language preferences from your profile settings. These preferences are stored in cookies.

⚠️ Important: If you disable essential cookies, you may not be able to log in to the platform or some features may not work properly.
